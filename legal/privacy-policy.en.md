# Tranzfr Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Tranzfr ("the App", "we") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Tranzfr mobile app.

Tranzfr is developed and operated by **Pablo** (independent developer), acting in his own name, pending formal registration of a business activity. For the purposes of this policy, "we" refers to this individual as the data controller.

**Contact:** pablo@traqcker.com

---

## 1. What data we collect

We only collect the data needed for the App to work:

- **Account data**: email and password (securely managed by our authentication provider, Supabase; we never store your password in plain text, and we cannot see it).
- **Display name**: the name your group members will see.
- **App usage data**: the groups you create or join, the expenses you log, the payments/settlements between members, and any notes you add to them.
- **Images**: if you choose to add a photo to a group, it is uploaded and stored associated with that group.
- **Language preference**: your chosen language (Spanish or English), stored locally on your device.

We do not collect location data, device contacts, or any advertising identifiers. The App contains no ads and no third-party analytics or tracking tools.

## 2. How we use your data

We use your data exclusively to:

- Create and manage your account.
- Show you the groups, expenses, and balances you take part in.
- Allow other members of your groups to see your name, expenses, and payments within that group (this is the App's core purpose: sharing expenses).
- Send you necessary transactional emails (account confirmation, password recovery).
- Keep the App secure and prevent abuse.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.

## 3. Where your data is stored

Data is stored on **Supabase** servers (our database, authentication, and file storage provider), acting as a data processor. Supabase applies its own security measures; you can review its policy at [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

## 4. Who we share your data with

- **Other members of your groups**: see your name, the expenses you log, and the payments you make within groups shared with you. This is inherent to how the App works.
- **Supabase**: as our infrastructure provider (database, authentication, storage, and transactional emails).
- **Authorities**: only if legally required.

We do not share your data with ad networks, data brokers, or any other third party.

## 5. Payments

The App currently does not process real payments: screens related to "unlocking features" are demonstration-only and no charge is made. If real payments are enabled in the future, this policy will be updated to detail the payment provider used (e.g. Stripe) and what payment data is processed (we will never store your full card details).

## 6. How long we keep your data

We keep your data while your account is active. If you request account deletion, we delete your personal data and any groups you solely own or are the only member of, unless the law requires us to keep it longer.

## 7. Your rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate data (you can change your display name from your profile).
- Request deletion of your account and data.
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
- Withdraw your consent at any time (which means you stop using the App).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **pablo@traqcker.com**. We will respond within a reasonable time and, in any case, within the limits set by applicable data protection law (UK GDPR / GDPR).

## 8. Children

The App is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under that age. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us to have it removed.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If changes are significant, we will notify you within the App. The "last updated" date at the top of this document always reflects the current version.

## 10. Contact

For any privacy or data protection questions, email **pablo@traqcker.com**.
