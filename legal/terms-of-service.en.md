## 1. What Tranzfr is

Tranzfr is a tool for organizing and splitting shared expenses among groups of people (trips, shared flats, etc.), calculating balances between members, and recording payments/settlements made outside the App.

**Important: Tranzfr does not transfer money.** The App only keeps track of who owes what to whom. Any actual payment between members (bank transfer, cash, etc.) happens outside the App, by whatever means the users choose; "recording a payment" in the App only updates the displayed balances — it does not execute or constitute a real transaction.
